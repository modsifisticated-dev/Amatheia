[[Thri-kreen]] a completely alien race that speaks using clicks and pheremones they live on a chain of islands in a difficult to navigate area of the sea. Their biology means they can never accurately speak common although those committed can understand it. Given enough time a humanoid companion would be able to identify certain desires based on clicks and smells but they would never truly be able to have a conversation. A Thri-kreen would be killed on sight if not accompanied by a more trustworthy race to vouch for their kind nature.
