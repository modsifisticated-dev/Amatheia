[[Dwarves]] are people carved from stone. They are mighty [[artisans]]: a dwarf's worst work would probably last [[Humans]] multiple generations. A new dwarf is born when one is carved from stone; each dwarf will only make four or so attempts at carving a dwarf in their lifetime, and often it is a collaboration with a loved one, although it does not have to be. [[Dwarves]] will leave their statues lining the walls of an active volcano, and often after an eruption the fires of [[Zahesh]] will leave several [[Dwarves]] sleeping where their statue lay. After the eruption, those who do not turn to a dwarf are kicked into the volcano as a sacrifice to [[Zahesh]] and a thank you.
