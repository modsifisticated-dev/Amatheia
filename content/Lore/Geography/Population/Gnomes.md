[[Gnomes]] are said to be favored by Eostre, they have trickster like natures and see the world as a cosmic joke being played on the living they take almost nothing seriously and often will laugh the hardest right as they die seeing the meaninglessness in the sadness of those around them. Gnomes are often good natured but will not hesitate to play a prank on anyone no matter their standing. Gnomish society is ranked based on who is funniest and as such their rulers are often bards who have told the funniest story anyone has heard.

Gnomes are born from eggs that take 5 years to hatch they come out almost fully developed with a personality. All the eggs hatch in the month of Gale (Eostre's month) the few gnomes that hatch outside of Gale are left to fend for themselves. Gnomes don't take ownership of their children and leave their eggs in communal hiding places. Usually a clutch of children will be left with a few mothers who reer the group for 6-14 months before they are able to join the wider Gnome community.
