During the time of the Mistfall many humanoids fled to move to safer ground eventually packing the city of skanley to the brim before the last spell. It's unknown if those humanoids who stayed behind were corrupted by the mist or if it caused some sort of excellarated evolution in the native animals of those islands but whatever the cause the outer islands of amatheia are now inhabited by the beastfolk races.

Many wondering beastfolk exist on the mainland but the outer islands are the only known beastman settlements

On Beastfolk:
A man with the features of a frog leapt from a tower the height of two buildings or more, narrowly avoiding the fishing lines of his companions as they cast from the same elevated perch. The isles were a safe place, but they were nothing but swamp. As such, his people had developed building techniques the mainlanders did not quite understand. Using mud and intricate rope joints, they created towers that, by all rights, should not have withstood even a strong wave, let alone the brutal weather known to the Amatheians. Yet, despite common logic, the beastfolk towers of the Grey Isles swayed in the wind, showing no signs of impeding collapse.

Eleswhere on the Sobolo Islands, the ram-like beastfolk sharpened their horns, preparing for a neighboring chieftain to take a crack at the throne. Despite constant battles over generations, these islands had never been unified under a single ruler. The people fought over grudges whose origins none could remember. Still, they fought on, for tradition held more weight than reason among them.
