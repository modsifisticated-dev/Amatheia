[[Orcs]] bond with their mates usually around 16 and will do almost everything in a pair in battle they prefer to fight side by side. The orcs speak in a rhythmic tone even when speaking common all emotion is expressed through music and tone of a voice it is almost impossible to read any emotion in an orcs face. but they atune to the frequencies of different rhythms they hear at all times. An orc doesn't have to atune to the frequecy they are truly feeling and have the ability to lie. Those who haven't spent much time with orcs would likely find it nearly impossible to read their emotions. Orcs are born to survive the harsh and arid areas of Amethia and if it wasn't for humans filling all the open cracks they would dominate this evolutionary niche
