[[Grung]] live in a swampy quagmire they have almost no land where they are able to build structures which means that the little area they can build has been built vertically to an unbeliveable extent they exist primarily off trade with the pirate fleet of errol buck but they serve as the first line of defence and warning if anyone tried to approach errols cove. If you approach their islands it wouldn't be strange to see Grung diving and fishing on 100 ft tall structures that sway in the wind
