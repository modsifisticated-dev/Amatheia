Elves have alien like communication skills most noticeable by the fact they laugh when most would cry and get upset when most would laugh. They are the first of the races to be born on Amethia and as such they hold an air of importance amongst themselves, additionally the length of time they live makes them the most stable society in Amethia, very few elves would remember a time before the mist although their histories chronicle times well before other races walked the earth.
