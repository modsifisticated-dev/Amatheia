[[Shota'ar]] is the ancestral homeland of the [[Orcs]], a previously peaceful people before the [[Humans]] gathered at [[Sa'ama]].

The [[Orcs]] of Shoshta'ar are a tribal people and value tradition above all else. Their ancestors gathered [[gemhearts]], which were used to create brilliant [[dyes]], [[magical wine]], [[perfumes]], and [[silks]] that glistened and reflected light in unbelievable ways.

Now the [[Orcs]] struggle to continue their [[traditions]] with the constant battles against the [[Humans]]. If they could only unite the [[Giff]] on the nearby islands to stand with them, they would have a chance.

The other benefit the [[Orcs]] have is an understanding of the pathways and sounds associated with [[The Banjax Desert]]. They often are first to arrive at a plateau, as they have heard the specific sounds of a pupating and know the quickest paths through the desert.
