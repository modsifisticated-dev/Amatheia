[[Waihitu]] is the trading hub of the continent and controls the major [[shipping lanes]] between all nations.

[[Waihitu]] is a nation of tanned sailors. They trade and travel the world. [[Waihitu]] attempts to remain completely neutral on all [[global conflicts]], as any uneasiness] with another nation could mean difficulty [[shipping]] around Amatheia.

[[Waihitu]] sustains its people on a majority of [[seafood]] and makes a decent trade in [[pearls]] and [[fireworks]], which are coveted as luxury goods in the rest of the world. [[Waihitu]] also produces the majority of [[ships]] for the rest of the world. With the largest standing naval army, currently used to protect its ocean [[shipping lanes]], [[Waihitu]] could be a considerable threat if pressed into a military conflict. Their biggest competitor and constant problem isthe pirate captain [[Errol Buck]], who plunders many of their unprotected [[ships]].

Almost all [[goods]] and [[money]] on the continent travels through [[Waihitu]], and as such they have many allies.
