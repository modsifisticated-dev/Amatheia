[[Hollowind]] is a melting pot of culture. There is no dominant race in [[Hollowind]]; it is filled with people who seek a free life and have come to find their way in a nation not focused on their background.

[[Hollowind]] is rare in that they hold democratic elections for their leaders.

[[Hollowind]] survives on the trade of [[tropical fruits]] they grow along the coastline, large [[clay]] deposits that allow them to manufacture [[pots]] and other [[homeware goods]] on a large scale, and consistent access to [[medical ingredients]] and [[magical ingredients]] from the [[Eltari Swamp]]. Most of the world's [[natural spell components]] grow naturally and are harvested locally in [[Eltari Swamp]] .

[[Hollowind]]'s points of power are:

The [[potions]] manufacturing hub of the world. Their great warehouses hold stocks of thousands of [[potions]] and [[magical ingredients]] which are often required to maintain a large community, such as those of the other major nations.

The [[Eltari Swamp]], which gives access to large quantities of natural magical sources.

The multicultural nature of the city, leading to a blending of cultures and skills.
