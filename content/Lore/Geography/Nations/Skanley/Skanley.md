[[Skanley]] was one of the last cities left standing when the [[Mistfall]] came. It is a long-standing nation that respects the rule of its emperor, given the long lives and timelines associated with the [[Elves]]. Its people prefer a single ruler who can make changes and plans that are consistent across the nation. The [[Elves]] have tried a democratic leader in the past, and often it leads to corruption and complacency. As such, the nobility of [[Skanley]] are raised from a young age with a strict [[education]] in [[military strategy]] for the first one hundred years, until they are old enough to rule.

The long-term planning of [[Skanley]] has led to them being a major power on the continent. All the youths of nobles study in [[Skanlian college]]s, and as such most people have some diplomatic association with the nation.

Additionally, the consistency of [[Skanley]] has led to it holding the central bank and maintaining a consistent [[currency]]. Strict regulations are held, and if a nation was found to be carving shavings off of their [[gold pieces]] to profit, [[Skanley]] would raise its army to stop them and likely be aided by many allies.

They also do a major trade in [[lamp oil]] made from blubber of whales and large marine creatures they hunt, [[ivory]] from the tusks of mammoths, and [[ice]] used to store and transport food.

The [[Dragonborn]] of Skanley are thankful for a place to call home, but there is growing unrest with the power structure of the city, as a dragonborn leader would be all but impossible.
