[[Kragan]] is the dwarf stronghold located in the mountain range of western Efora [[The Whistling Teeth]] (Placeholder name).

The [[Dwarves]] retreated to their mountain for safety during the mist, and were the only major race to not concede their homeland to the mist. As a result, [[dwarvish culture]] remains strong and traditional. [[Dwarves]] are great craftsmen and will create finer [[tools]] than those of any other race. Despite the lack of local [[natural resources]], a dwarf craftsman will often be the head of production in the different nations of [[Efora]], or resources will be shipped to [[Kragan]] for specific artisanal needs. They often do not do production on the scale required to outfit an army, but for a king's personal armor or retinue, [[Kragan craftsmen]] are second to none.

The world's [[metal ore]] is located in [[Kragan]], which, after [[Amatheia]] bounced back enough for people to raise armies, has led to [[Kragan]] being a major exporting nation. They also make minor trade in yaks and other [[beasts of burden]] located in the mountains, and [[mushrooms]] that grow in great underground groves.

The only points of power in [[Kragan]] are the city of Kragan itself and the smaller [[metal ore]] mines in the neighbouring mountains.

The mountainous region of Kragan, combined with the expertise of its people, has made [[Kragan]] nearly impossible to assault.
