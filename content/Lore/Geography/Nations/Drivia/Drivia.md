[[Drivia]] as a nation has been under constant threat from magic attack since the receeding of the [[Mistfall]].

[[Issachar]], a great [[lich]], controlled the region for longer than most mortals can remember. Then he was dethroned by [[Alaric the Cursed]], who continued to pillage the land.

Currently, [[Drivia]] is ruled by [[King Rolandus the Stubborn]].

The people who have remained in [[Drivia]] for generations are survivors and are used to surviving on their own without help from their king. [[Drivians]] are a hearty people who will not hesitate to share a meal, but will never put themselves at risk of starving.

The capital of Drivia, the castle in the south, is one of the few structures to stand after generations of conflict.

[[Drivia]] benefits from the constant conflict in Vrayish by having access to a plethora of lost magic items. Almost all the most powerful magic items in [[Amatheia]] are contained somewhere in the [[dungeons of Vrayish]], and as such [[Drivia]] benefits from the trade of these items and those who need supplying to search for them.

[[Drivia]] also benefits from a strong metal trade with [[Sa'ama]] and forge their metals into [[weapons]] and [[armor]] to outfit the king's private military and trade with the rest of the world.

The points of power in [[Drivia]] would be the port opposite Waithitu, which gives them access to the trading hub of Efora.

Another point of power would be the villages that farm the forest to the east for [[syrup]]s, which make a hefty trade, as conventional sugar cane growing is only possible on small scale.

Lastly is the [[Himont mountain ranges]], which give [[Vrayish]] a strong defencible position, being unassailable from the west.
