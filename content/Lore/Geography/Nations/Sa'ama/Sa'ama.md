The people of [[Sa'ama]] fight for a prize in [[The Banjax Desert]]: the prize they fight for are [[gemhearts]], the crystalline hearts of a specific creature that pupates on [[The Banjax Desert]]. These hearts are worth 500,000 gp, but rarely are they sold. They are more regularly used as spell components for resurrections, for which they are worth more than their weight in gems.

The people of [[Sa'ama]] are a desert people hardened by war. They settled this region after [[the mistfall]], as this region was left mostly unclaimed, and the previously nomadic trading Sa'amans found a source of wealth in the desert. The only problem is the orcs of [[Shota'ar]], who fight to claim these [[gemhearts]] for themselves.

The places of power for [[Sa'ama]] are:

The crystal capital of Sa'ama '[[the city of glass]]' (placeholder name), where legendary [[glassblowers]] and [[gemcrafters]] gather to create some of the finest works of [[art]] from [[gemhearts]] and the sand that surrounds them.

The constant war fuels the endless [[metal forging]]. [[Sa'ama]] refines almost all the [[metal]] being shipped from [[Kragan]] and have forged a strong alliance with the dwarves; their craftsmen oversee much of the [[smelting]]. This [[metal]] is then quickly forged into [[simple weapons]] or shipped to [[Drivia]] to be crafted into more complex and valuable weaponry.

The small military camps of [[Sa'aman local mercenaries]] that surround [[Sa'ama]] produce some of the finest militia on all of [[Amatheia]], and as such those looking for a life of fighting come to [[Sa'ama]] to train and test their steel. These camps sometimes secure a [[gemheart]] for themselves and trade it to '[[the city of glass]]' (placeholder name) for a fee.
