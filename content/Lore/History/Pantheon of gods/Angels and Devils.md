devils and demons are created when enough anguish has been caused by a person a demon will be born of the hatred aimed towards them. Devils are the higher ranking in hell and are often assigned to those who have caused much hatred but with a purpose or noble cause. Demons are lesser and are assigned to those with evil or less noble causes of hatred. When a person with a sufficient amount of hatred dies they are sent through the nine hells each tier a punishment that once endured and learned from can continue to the next. a demon or devil is their personal guide through this realm, when they reach the end those who have atoned and stand by their actions in life are allowed into heaven while those who don't stand by their actions or haven't sufficiently learned from their punishments are sent back to Amethia and reincarnated

Similarly, Angels and guardians are born when someone inspires enough love from those around them, often it will be associated with a specific god of man or elemental god and be born of the specific prayers of a person or those around them. A person doesn't need to be inherently devout to inspire the creation of an angel for example a hero could inspire several angels without ever stopping to pray themselves.

People who inspire the creation of both one or more angels or devils will be born onto a battlefield after death in which they participate in a fight to determine the outcome of their soul it's not unheard of for people to fight on the side of the devils if they believe there is still more for them to do on Amethia, these people often find themselves passing through the nine hells quite quickly. If the Angels succeed in this combat the person being fought over is often brought outside of the realm of the dreams of the all-father to an eternal afterlife free from fear of it all ending when the all-father wakes.
