The Pirate fleet of captain errol buck calls port somewhere in the Eltari swamp near [[Hollowind]]  some say he has a hidden cove or cave where the fleet resides. His people are baiyoo people they come from the swamp and his signature flag shows it with the head of a crocodile bearing a nasty grin

Errols fleet isn't inherently evil they take just enough to live a comfortable life on the seas often they won't even fully raid a ship as leaving it plunder might cause a nation to raise a naval army or them to go out of buisness

Errol believes in throwing back the little fish to catch another day
