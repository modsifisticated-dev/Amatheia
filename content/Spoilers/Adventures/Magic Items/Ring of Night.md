The ring of night is made from a dull gray metal and radiates
magic. It has a batlike motif, with the following phrase written
on the inside of the band in magical script (requiring a read
magic spell to translate): Defend, deceive, destroy. It expands
or shrinks to fit any size hand from giant to leprechaun. The ring
of nights powers increase as it gets closer to the base of
Cabilars lair. Therefore, when the ring is on dungeon level III,
it is more powerful than when it is found on level I. 

Here are its
powers:
Power / Where it functions / Command word
AC +1, Saving Throws +1 / Prime Material Plane / None
60 Darkvision (or to 120 if wearer has Darkvision naturally) / 10-mile radius of Cabilars lair / None
No need to eat or breathe / Cabilars tower and dungeon / None
Cast False Life twice per day / Dungeon levels I, II, III  / Power one
Cast Burning hands at 5th level of ability, / twice per day Dungeon levels II, III / Power two
Cast Magic missile at 5th level of ability, / three times per day / Dungeon level III Power three
